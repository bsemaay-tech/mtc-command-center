# Risks And Unknowns

## Risks
- EMA channel crossings may lag trend reversals.
- Pullback-to-EMA-channel systems can whipsaw in ranges.
- Timeframe and market are missing.
- "Touch", "inside", and "close back above/below" can change results materially.
- Fixed 2R target may not fit every market/regime.
- Marketing claim of high accuracy is unsupported.

## Unknowns
- Exact video URL.
- Channel name and ID.
- Market and timeframe used in examples.
- Whether entries are at signal close or next open.
- Whether stops are intrabar hard stops or close-based.
- Whether the green channel uses high/close/low as three separate lines or upper/lower bounds only.

## Required Clarifications Before Backtest
- Source URL/video ID.
- Target market and timeframe.
- Exact channel-cross and pullback semantics.
- Execution timing and stop behavior.
