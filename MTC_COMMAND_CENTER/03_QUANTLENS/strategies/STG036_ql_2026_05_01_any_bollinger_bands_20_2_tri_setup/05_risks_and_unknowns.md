# Risks And Unknowns

## Main Risks
- Band-width thresholds are not specified.
- "Sideways market" is discretionary unless formalized.
- "Strong candle" is not defined.
- Reversal setup can fail badly in wide/trending regimes.
- Breakout setup can suffer false breakouts without follow-through criteria.
- Support/resistance and trendlines can introduce lookahead if built from future pivots.

## Unknowns
- Exact source video URL.
- Channel name and channel ID.
- Market and timeframe used in examples.
- Stop-loss and take-profit rules for each setup.
- Whether band touches require close outside band or wick contact only.

## Mitigations
- Start with event extraction.
- Use past-only volatility and range rules.
- Keep setup variants separate.
- Label as research-only until tested.
