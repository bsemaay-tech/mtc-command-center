## Approach
Concentrate in **theme leaders** where fundamentals AND technicals corroborate;
hold for the big move; manage risk with staged stops.

## Setups / modules
- **LEADERSHIP_THEME_SCORE** — rank by theme leadership (AI/semis/energy/crypto etc.),
  fundamentals confirmed by technicals.
- **50D_RECLAIM_TIGHT_ACTION** — entry on reclaim of the 50DMA with **tight** price
  action (contraction) after a base.
- **EXTENSION_OVER_50D_HEDGE_SIGNAL** — when price is **too far above the 50DMA**,
  trim/hedge (overextension).
- **357_SCALEOUT_RISK_MODULE** — staged stop on a new position: exit/scale at
  **3% / 5% / 7%** against entry depending on conviction/context. Core risk rule.
- **POSITION_TRADING_HOLD_ENGINE** — once proven, **hold the leader** through the trend
  (don't trade out of the big winner early).

## Entry / risk / exit skeleton
- **Entry:** theme leader reclaims 50DMA with tight action.
- **Risk:** 357 staged stop on new positions; sell/hedge on extension over 50DMA.
- **Hold:** ride leaders; only exit on trend/character break or 50DMA loss.

## Reusable components for MTC
- Theme-leadership score, 50DMA reclaim + tightness entry, extension-over-50DMA trim,
  357 staged stop (money management), position hold engine.

## Lessons (keep)
- Big money is made by **holding leaders**, not trading around them too much.
- Cut new positions fast (3-5-7%) but give proven winners room.
- Fundamentals must be **corroborated by the chart** before committing size.

## Risks for backtesting
- Theme/leadership scoring is discretionary → encode RS + group rank objectively.
- Needs fundamental data for full fidelity; technical-only is an approximation.
