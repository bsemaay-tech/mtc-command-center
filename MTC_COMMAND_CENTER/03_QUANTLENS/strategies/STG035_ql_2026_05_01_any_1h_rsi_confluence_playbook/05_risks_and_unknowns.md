# Risks And Unknowns

## Risks
- Divergence and trendline rules can be highly lookahead-prone.
- Too many combinations can overfit quickly.
- Support/resistance and Fibonacci swing selection are discretionary.
- RSI overbought/oversold behavior changes in strong trends.
- "Any market/timeframe" claim is unvalidated.

## Unknowns
- Video URL and channel metadata.
- RSI default length is implied but not explicitly locked in the transcript.
- Moving average type on RSI is described as moving average but not fully specified.
- Exact support/resistance construction.
- Exact Fibonacci swing rule.
- Exact pivot windows for divergence and trendlines.

## Required Clarifications Before Backtest
- Select first variant.
- Lock RSI length and MA type.
- Lock timeframe and market.
- Define pivot and confluence rules without future leakage.
- Separate reversal and continuation variants in reports.
