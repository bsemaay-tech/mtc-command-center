## Entry (fully mechanizable part)
- Stock forms a **base** (cup / cup-with-handle / flat base) in a stage-2 uptrend.
- Identify the **pivot point** (ideal buy point = base/handle high).
- **Buy exactly at the pivot** on the breakout (ideally on volume expansion).

## Sell rules (the core, deterministic)
- **Capital-protection stop: sell if it drops 7-8% below the buy point, no questions.**
- **Profit goal: +20-25% from the standard base pivot** (take/trim there unless a
  rare hold exception applies).
- Don't change rules intraweek; follow them mechanically.
