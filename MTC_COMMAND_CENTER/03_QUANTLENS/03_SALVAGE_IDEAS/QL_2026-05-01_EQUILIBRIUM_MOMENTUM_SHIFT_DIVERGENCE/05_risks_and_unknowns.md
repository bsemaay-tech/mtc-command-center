# Risks And Unknowns

## Risks
- External indicator dependency.
- Formula incompleteness.
- Unknown repaint behavior in divergence labels.
- Discretionary divergence pivot selection.
- No target/exit model.
- Momentum divergence can lead only to pullback, not full reversal.

## Unknowns
- Video URL and channel metadata.
- Exact BigBeluga indicator source.
- Exact nonlinear compression function.
- Exact smoothing lengths.
- Market and timeframe.
- Target and exit logic after entry.

## Required Clarifications Before Any Further Work
- Provide source URL/video ID.
- Provide exact TradingView script link or Pine source.
- Define target/exit rules.
- Define divergence pivot timing.
