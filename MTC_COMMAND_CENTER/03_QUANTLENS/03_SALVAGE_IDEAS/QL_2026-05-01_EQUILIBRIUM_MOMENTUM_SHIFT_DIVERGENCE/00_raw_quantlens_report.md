## Transcript-Derived Summary
The video introduces `Equilibrium Momentum Shift`, a TradingView oscillator by BigBeluga. It computes an equilibrium price from the midpoint of the highest high and lowest low over a selected range. Price deviation from this midpoint is smoothed, normalized by range size, and compressed into a bounded oscillator.

The transcript describes two use modes:
- Momentum/signal line crosses, similar to MACD.
- Divergence analysis using the momentum line, confirmed by price-action structure breaks.

The tradeable portion is the divergence confirmation framework. A bearish divergence needs a close below the swing low between highs before short entry. A bullish divergence needs a close above the swing high between lows before long entry.
