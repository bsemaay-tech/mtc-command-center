# Dashboard Tabs

## 1. Home / Project Overview

Shows product phase, warnings, latest reports, and next recommended tasks.

## 2. AI Task Board

Shows queued, active, blocked, failed, and completed AI tasks.

## 3. Parity Matrix

Shows parity case status, pass/fail counts, repeated failure patterns, and report links.

## 4. Case Explorer

Shows individual case metadata, inputs, expected outputs, and diagnostic history.

## 5. Backtest Lab

Shows latest backtest runs, metrics, logs, and failure summaries.

## 6. Optimization Lab

Shows parameter experiments, comparison summaries, and promotion candidates.

## 7. QuantLens Intake

Shows transcript/research candidates, intake review status, and missing user inputs.

## 8. Strategy Registry

Shows known strategies, lifecycle status, evidence level, and promotion readiness.

## 9. Pine Builder

Shows Pine drafts, review status, compile observations, and promotion gates.

## 10. Reports Center

Shows generated reports by category with task IDs, dates, and summaries.

## 11. Data Health

Shows data sources, export freshness, missing files, and schema warnings.

## 12. Worker Monitor

Shows AI worker roles, current task ownership, and recent handoffs.

## 13. LiveOps Sandbox

Shows future dry-run signal state only. Live trading and webhooks remain disabled.

## 14. Settings / Paths

Shows configured roots, status paths, report paths, and safety flags.
