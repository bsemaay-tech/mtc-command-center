# Gemini Task Template: Research Strategy Task

## Purpose

Research an external trading idea and prepare a structured intake note for MCC.

## Inputs

- Source URL, transcript, paper, or user brief
- Market/timeframe context
- Any user constraints

## Output

- Strategy summary
- Claimed edge
- Required indicators and data
- Risks and assumptions
- Backtest questions
- QuantLens intake recommendation

## Safety

Do not present external claims as verified. Mark unverifiable claims clearly.
