# Gemini Task Template: Internet Strategy Scan Task

## Purpose

Scan current online strategy ideas only when the task explicitly requests internet research.

## Workflow

1. Record search scope and date.
2. Prefer primary sources and credible technical references.
3. Summarize claims without copying long source text.
4. Identify testable rules.
5. Separate hype from implementable logic.
6. Recommend next research action.

## Safety

Do not store credentials, private data, or paid content. Do not claim profitability without local verification.
